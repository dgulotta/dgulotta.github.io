Perfectoid Spaces (Summer 2019)

Instructor
Daniel Gulotta
Location and time
Monday and Thursday 12pm-1pm in room C3, with some make-up sessions Tuesday 12pm-1pm; see below.
Prerequisites
I will assume some familiarity with p-adic numbers and with algebraic geometry.

References

Main references:

  • [SW1] P. Scholze, J. Weinstein, Berkeley lectures on p-adic geometry
  • [SW2] P. Scholze, J. Weinstein, Moduli of p-divisible groups
  • [W] J. Weinstein, Arizona Winter School 2017: Adic spaces

Additional references for the local Langlands part of the last lecture:

  • P. Colmez, G. Dospinescu, W. Niziol, Cohomologie p-adique de la tour de Drinfeld: le cas de la dimension 1
  • T. Gee, Modularity lifting theorems (section 2.6)
  • M. Harris, Supercuspidal representations in the cohomology of Drinfelʹd upper half spaces; elaboration of Carayol's program
  • P. Scholze, On the p-adic cohomology of the Lubin-Tate tower
  • T. Wedhorn, The local Langands correspondence for GL(n) over p-adic fields
