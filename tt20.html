p-adic Hodge theory (Summer 2020)

Instructor
Daniel Gulotta
Location and time
Tuesday and Thursday 3:30pm-4:30pm, online. The password is the number of roots of unity in Q34759.
Prerequisites
I will assume some familiarity with p-adic numbers and with algebraic geometry.

Notes will be posted here. I do not plan to give homework, but you may find it helpful to do some of the exercises in references [BC] and [D] listed below.

References

  • [B] L. Berger, An introduction to the theory of p-adic representations
  • [BC] O. Brinon and B. Conrad, CMI summer school notes on p-adic Hodge theory
  • [BMS] B. Bhat, M. Morrow, P. Scholze, Integral p-adic Hodge theory
  • [BS] B. Bhatt and P. Scholze, Prisms and prismatic cohomology
  • [C] P. Colmez, Une construction de BdR+
  • [D] H. Diao, Period rings and period sheaves
  • [F] J.-M. Fontaine, Formes différentielles et modules de Tate des variétés abéliennes sur les corps locaux
  • [FO] J.-M. Fontaine and L. Ouyang, Theory of p-adic Galois representations
  • [K] K. Kedlaya, New methods for (φ,Γ)-modules
  • [LB] A.-C. Le Bras, Overconvergent de Rham cohomology over the Fargues-Fontaine curve
  • [SW] P. Scholze and J. Weinstein, Berkeley lectures on p-adic geometry
  • [T] J. Tate, p-divisible groups
  • [W] J. Weinstein, Arizona Winter School 2017: Adic spaces
